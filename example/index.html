Radical Gallery

Protest art itself doesn’t create change, but it aims to embolden and galvanize enough people across socio-economic backgrounds to mobilize for a cause.

—Deva Pardue, graphic designer

Protest art is an important tool of activists and social movements. It can inform and inspire. It comes in a variety of shapes and forms: signs, banners, posters, and other printed or hand-made material.
